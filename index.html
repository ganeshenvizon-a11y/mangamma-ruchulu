Our Heritage

A Legacy of Rich Taste & Authenticity

For more than eleven years, Mangamma Ruchulu has stood as a beacon of culinary preservation. Founded with the mission to recreate the robust flavors of home-cooked Andhra and Telangana food, every recipe represents generations of kitchen secrets.

2015

Inception & Heritage Recipes

Opened our first doors with 25 heritage recipes, curated by traditional family cooks.

2020

Expanding the Kitchen

Broadened our menu to introduce rare, rustic local culinary gems from Telangana districts.

Present

Traditional Feast Redefined

Consistently voted as the premier location for genuine, uncompromising South Indian meals.

01. Signature Experience

Aritaku Bhojanam

The ultimate Andhra experience. A lavish assortment of traditional vegetable fries, dals, rasam, sambar, fresh chutneys, and hot rice drizzled with pure cow ghee, served traditionally on a fresh green banana leaf.

Explore Meal Details
02. Royal Heritage

Chicken Dum Biryani

Fragrant long-grained Basmati rice layered with tender chunks of chicken, infused with saffron, mint, and freshly ground handpicked spices, slow-cooked in sealed clay handis on charcoal fire.

Explore Biryani Details
03. Coastal Delicacy

Apollo Fish

Crispy, deep-fried fresh fish fillets tossed in a fiery, aromatic tempering of fresh curry leaves, green chilies, yogurt, and home-crafted spices. The perfect starter to ignite your palate.

Explore Seafood Details
04. Local Street Art

Chicken Majestic

A Hyderabad classic. Tender chicken strips marinated with buttermilk, lightly battered and stir-fried with green chilies, garlic, and special spices for an incredibly succulent bite.

Explore Starters Details
05. Rustic Health

Ragi Sangati

Traditional nutritious finger millet dumplings from the heart of Rayalaseema, served warm and best paired with our spicy country chicken curry (Natu Kodi Pulusu) and melted ghee.
